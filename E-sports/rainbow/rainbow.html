Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege

Intro

om Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is a first-person shooter game, in which players utilize many different operators from the Rainbow team. Different operators have different nationalities, weapons, and gadgets.The game features an asymmetrical structure whereby the teams are not always balanced in their choices of abilities.

Developed by Ubisoft MontReal & Published by Ubisoft.

It was released worldwide for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4,Xbox One,PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X.
